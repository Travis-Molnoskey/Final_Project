Background Info

This application was built to inform consumers on the important aspects of buying a diamond. Our goal was to help inform diamond buyers on how far their money will go and what kind of traits they should be looking for. Our machine learning model analyzes our database of 50000+ diamonds and determines the expected price for the given diamond traits in the context of the Four C's: Carat, Cut, Color and Clarity. An additional database search will compare a user input price point to different entries to give expected traits based on that price. Data analysis we've done also show the given trends across diamond traits and prices.
